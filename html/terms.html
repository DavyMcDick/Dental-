Our System facilitates dental appointment scheduling for participating dental practices. It incorporates a Shortest Job Next (SJN) approach to prioritize walk-in patients. This means that if a walk-in patient’s required procedure is assessed as the quickest compared to scheduled procedures, they may receive immediate treatment.

Scheduled Appointments: Users can book appointments in advance through our System. You are responsible for providing accurate personal and appointment-related information.

Walk-In Service (SJN):

  • Walk-in patients may be granted immediate treatment if their dental procedure is determined to be the quickest among all scheduled treatments.
  • The clinic will assess each situation based on current workload and available resources.
  • By choosing walk-in service, you agree that this priority may override previously scheduled appointments if your procedure is the fastest.
